# Southcentral PA Covid-19 tracking

Governor Wolf's [Plan to reopen Pennsylvania](https://www.governor.pa.gov/process-to-reopen-pennsylvania/) includes 
a threshold of new cases over 14 days of fewer than 50 cases per 100,000 residents in the region. The chart below
illustrates the number of new cases per 100,000 residents over the past 14 days in the Southcentral region, which 
includes Adams, Bedford, Blair, Cumberland, Dauphin, Franklin, Fulton, Huntingdon, Juniata, Lebanon, Mifflin, Perry, 
and York counties.
